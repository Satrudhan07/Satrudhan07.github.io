01 / 06
● Always listening

Silent, invisible, always ready

Buddy runs in the background using almost no resources. No dock icon, no taskbar clutter — just a tiny indicator that it's alive and waiting for you.

💡 Buddy listens for its name entirely on your device — your audio never leaves your computer before you've said "Hey Buddy".
02 / 06
● Listening

Say it. Buddy hears you.

The moment you say "Hey Buddy", the pill appears and starts transcribing your words in real time — so you can see exactly what it heard before it acts.

🎙️ Your voice is converted to text instantly on your own device. Nothing is sent anywhere just to understand what you said.
03 / 06
● Routing

Smart routing, instant decision.

Before anything leaves your device, Buddy's local AI decides — is this private? Handle it here. Is this complex? Send it to the cloud with your personal details removed.

🧠 Buddy's local AI brain makes a routing decision in a fraction of a second — entirely on your computer, before anything leaves your device.
04 / 06
● Local · Private

Private requests stay private.

Home control, personal calendar, files, health data — anything sensitive is handled entirely on your device. The green shield means your data never left your machine.

🛡️ Around 80% of everyday requests are handled right on your device — no internet required, no data sent, no ongoing cost.
05 / 06
● Speaking

Buddy speaks back. You see every word.

As Buddy talks, the pill shows a live subtitle of the response — word by word. Works perfectly in noisy environments, or when you just want to read along.

💬 Buddy speaks back to you using a voice engine that runs entirely on your device. The live subtitle isn't a gimmick — it's an accessibility feature built in from day one.
06 / 06
● Cloud · Active

When cloud is needed, your data is scrubbed.

For complex questions that need cloud reasoning, Buddy removes your personal details before sending — then fills them back in when the response arrives.

☁️ Buddy's privacy filter automatically removes your name, location, and any personal details before anything reaches the cloud. "Book a flight for Sarah" becomes "Book a flight for [PERSON]" — then Buddy fills the name back in when the answer arrives.

Just say "Hey Buddy"

No clicking, no typing, no switching apps. Wake word detection means Buddy is always one phrase away — silently listening, never recording.

🧠
Encrypted memory

Buddy learns your preferences, allergies, work hours, and more — stored in military-grade encrypted files on your device. Never synced. Always yours.

💤
Always ready, never intrusive

No dock icon when idle. No menu bar clutter. Just a tiny breathing pill that appears when you speak and vanishes when you're done.

Live response subtitles

Every spoken response is subtitled in real time inside the pill — an accessibility feature and a convenience feature in one, built in from day one.

Only your voice

Buddy learns to recognise your voice and only responds to you. Your voice profile lives on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.
